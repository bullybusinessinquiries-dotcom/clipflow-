Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Overview

ClipFlow is designed with privacy at its core. This policy explains how data is handled when you use the Tool. The short version: your content stays on your machine.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

ClipFlow does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your video or audio content
  • Your transcripts or clip metadata
  • Your API credentials or access tokens
  • Your usage patterns, analytics, or telemetry
  • Any personal information about your audience

3. Local Processing

All video transcription is performed entirely on your local machine using OpenAI Whisper, an open-source model. No audio or video data is sent to any external server for transcription. Processing happens 100% offline after initial model download.

4. API Credentials

Your TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Twitter/X credentials are stored locally in environment variables on your machine. They are never transmitted to ClipFlow or any third party. You are solely responsible for keeping these credentials secure.

5. Platform APIs

When the Tool uploads clips to social media platforms, it communicates directly with those platforms' official APIs. This data transfer is governed by each platform's own privacy policy.

6. Log Files

The Tool generates local log files on your machine recording run times, clip counts, upload status, and errors. These logs are stored only on your computer and are never transmitted externally. You may delete them at any time.

7. Email Alerts

If you configure email alerts, your email address is stored in your local environment file. Summary emails are sent directly from your configured email provider using SMTP. ClipFlow does not store or process your email address.

8. Data Retention

Raw video files are automatically deleted from your machine after processing. Transcript files and clip metadata are retained locally for your reference. You control all data on your own machine and may delete any files at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

ClipFlow is intended for use by content creators and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the Tool after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through our TikTok profile or GitHub repository.
